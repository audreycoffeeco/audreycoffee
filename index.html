01 — About

Some places are meant to be experienced slowly. Audréy opened in 2021 with a simple idea - that coffee, light, and quiet still matter.

Five Elephant Coffee House Baked Breakfast & Lunch Dog Friendly

Tucked between the linden trees of Holtzendorffstraße, the space is small and intentionally simple.

We serve specialty coffee using beans from Five Elephant, alongside artisan cakes, cookies, and pastries.

Breakfast and lunch stay focused on classic dishes, made with quality and regional ingredients.

Everything is made fresh, with a focus on good ingredients and simple execution.
